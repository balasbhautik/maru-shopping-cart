About Us

Maru Shopping Cart

Maru Shopping Cart is a Gujarat e-commerce company, headquartered in Junagadh, Rajkot, Surat, and incorporated in Ahmedabad as a private limited company. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products.
